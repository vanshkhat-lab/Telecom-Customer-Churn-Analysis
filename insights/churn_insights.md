## High-Risk Customer Segments
- Customers on month-to-month contracts
- Customers with tenure less than 12 months
- Fiber optic service users
- Customers with high monthly charges
- Customers without tech support

## Churn Drivers
- Lack of long-term commitment
- Poor early customer experience
- High service pricing
- Limited support services

## Business Recommendations
- Encourage customers to switch to yearly contracts
- Improve engagement during the first year
- Offer personalized pricing plans
- Bundle support services with internet plans

## Conclusion
By identifying high-risk churn segments and analyzing customer behavior using SQL,
targeted retention strategies can be implemented to reduce customer churn and
increase long-term customer value.
